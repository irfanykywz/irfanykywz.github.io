## Overview

Bleed builds are powerful and flexible. This guide focuses on early accessibility and smooth scaling into mid–late game.

## Starting Class & Early Gear

- Bandit or Samurai for early Dex/Arcane.
- Pick up `Twinblade` in Dragon-Burnt Ruins cellar.
- Farm `Rivers of Blood` later; use `Uchigatana` + `Blood Slash` first.

## Ashes of War

- `Bloody Slash` early game.
- Transition to `Seppuku` on twinblades or katanas when available.

## Talismans

- `Lord of Blood’s Exultation` (Leyndell catacombs).
- `Rotten Winged Sword Insignia` for increased damage on successive hits.

## Route Highlights

1. Limgrave → pick up Flask upgrades, map fragments.
2. Caelid early for decent runes; avoid big fights.
3. Liurnia → Bell Bearings for smithing.

## Boss Tips

- Keep aggression high to proc bleed quickly.
- Learn 2–3 safe punish windows; don’t overcommit.

## Final Notes

Respec into more Arcane once Seppuku is online to maximize bleed.
